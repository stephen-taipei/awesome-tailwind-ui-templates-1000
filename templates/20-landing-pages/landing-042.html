Our Story

Culinary Excellence Since 1998

Founded by Chef Marco Rossi, Savory began as a humble dream to bring authentic, rustic Italian flavors to the heart of the city. Over the decades, we have evolved into a landmark for fine dining, maintaining our commitment to traditional techniques while embracing modern innovation.

Every dish tells a story of heritage, sourcing only the freshest produce from local farmers and premium imports directly from Italy.
